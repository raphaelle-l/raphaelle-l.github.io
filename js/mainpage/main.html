About Me

I’m a student at CU Boulder, studying Creative Technology and Design Engineering (CTDE). My love for creativity and problem-solving is what drew me to this field. I enjoy working on projects that combine technology and design to create meaningful and innovative solutions.

In addition to my studies, I’m a pastry chef with over five years of experience. Baking has always been a passion of mine, and I’ve spent years honing my skills in crafting desserts and baked goods.

I’m also a painter and enjoy using color and texture to bring my ideas to life. Painting is a way for me to express myself and explore new ways of seeing the world.
